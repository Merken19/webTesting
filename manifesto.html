Manifesto

I believe in the World Wide Web. Not in corpo-owned, multibillion worth, robot-operated, heartless websites. We all use those, heck we even need those to exist in today's semi-digital world. But this is not the real World Wide Web.

The Web I believe has lines all over the globe, connecting everybody. It is not condensed into Facebook(Meta) and TikTok to ship like an Amazon facility. It is free.

Hence, I created this website to exist in the virtual world with no chains attached to those corporations. I am free to share what I want, I am free to design how I want, I am free to be me.

However, this is not enough of the World Wide Web. The Web is not defined in singularity. It needs connections, we need connections. The ideal Web connects sites and lets people go. It doesn't try to keep you inside, it lets you surf the Internet.


I want you to be a part of the Web. I want you to not kick the Dead Internet. I want you to be aware and awake of your virtual existence. So, i want you to have your website.


I am running this website freely on GitHub Pages, with lots of help from html5up.net!
